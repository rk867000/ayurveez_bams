Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a course? +

1. Choose your course/subject from the Courses page
2. Make payment via UPI (ayurveez@oksbi) or bank transfer
3. Send payment screenshot to WhatsApp: 8271890090
4. Mention your name and course purchased
5. Receive access code within 2 hours
6. Enter code on dashboard to access content

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept:
• UPI payments (any UPI app: GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
• Bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS/RTGS)
• Mobile wallet payments

After payment, please send screenshot to WhatsApp for immediate activation.

How long do I get access to purchased courses? +

• Subject-wise purchase: 1 year access
• Complete Proff purchase: 2 years access
• Lifetime access available for complete BAMS package

All access periods start from the date of payment confirmation.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied? +

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if:
1. You haven't accessed more than 20% of the course content
2. The request is made within 7 days of purchase
3. Technical issues cannot be resolved

Refund requests must be sent via email to ayurveez@gmail.com with payment details.

How do doubt-solving calls work? +

Doubt-solving calls are available for enrolled students:
1. First Professional: 2 calls included with complete package
2. Second Professional: 3 calls included with complete package
3. Final Professional: 5 calls included with complete package

To schedule a call, message on WhatsApp with your preferred time and topics.

Do you provide certificates? +

Yes! We provide digital certificates for:
• Completion of any complete Professional package
• Scoring above 70% in final assessment tests

Certificates are issued in PDF format with unique ID and can be verified online.
